Wallet Address Screening

Blockchain analytic firms, such as Chainalysis, are currently checking your wallet address against the Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) sanctions list, as well as datasets that flag wallets associated with ransomware, hacks, fraud, terrorist financing, or other illicit activity

This screening is implemented via API-based risk scoring and sanctions monitoring. It is not identity-based KYC. It is address-based screening. If an address is determined to be a sanctioned entity, it is blocked from further interacting with the interface.
